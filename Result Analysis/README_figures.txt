IEEE Figures Kit
------------------
Files:
- results_comparison.csv
- cluster_metrics.csv
- irt_difficulty.csv
- figs_plot.m

How to use:
1) Download all four files to the same folder.
2) Open MATLAB and run:  run('figs_plot.m')
3) Three PNGs will be created:
   - fig_classification_metrics.png
   - fig_cluster_coherence.png
   - fig_irt_difficulty.png
